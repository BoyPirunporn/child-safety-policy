# Child Safety & CSAE Prevention Policy

_Last updated: 2025_

## 1. Commitment to Child Safety
Our app is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users, especially minors. We strictly prohibit any form of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) and Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM). This includes the creation, sharing, distribution, or promotion of illegal or harmful content involving minors.

## 2. Prohibited Content
We do not allow:
- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
- Sexualized content involving minors
- Grooming, exploitation, or attempts to lure minors
- Any content encouraging or enabling CSAE
- Accounts or behaviors that pose risks to child safety

Any violative content is subject to immediate removal.

## 3. Content Moderation & Enforcement
We use a combination of:
- User reporting tools
- Manual review by moderators
- Automated detection and prevention mechanisms (where applicable)

All reports involving child safety are reviewed with the highest priority.

## 4. Reporting Mechanisms
Users can report suspicious content or behavior through:
- In-app reporting tools
- Email contact: **prachyarak@gmail.com**

We encourage users to report any potential risks to children immediately.

## 5. Cooperation with Authorities
We comply with all applicable child safety laws in relevant regions.  
Confirmed or suspected CSAM incidents will be:
- Immediately removed
- Investigated
- Reported to appropriate national or regional authorities and organizations responsible for child protection

## 6. Account Actions
Violations of this policy may result in:
- Content removal
- Feature restrictions
- Temporary or permanent account suspension
- Reporting to law enforcement agencies

## 7. Protection of Minors
We take measures to prevent unauthorized contact with minors, including:
- Preventing inappropriate communication
- Blocking accounts that pose risks
- Monitoring patterns indicative of grooming or exploitation

## 8. Policy Updates
This policy may be updated as required to comply with new laws, improve safety measures, or enhance user protection. All updates will be publicly accessible on this page.
